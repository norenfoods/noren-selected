The Alliance

What is
Noren Selected?

Noren Selected is a curated collective of the world's finest extra virgin olive oils — handpicked by Norenfoods to enter China together, with full market support at every step.

01
Curated Entry
Every member passes our rigorous selection standard — combining international award records, GACC compliance, and production quality. Not all producers qualify. That exclusivity is the point.
02
China Localization
Via our BrandBank service, selected producers receive a complete Chinese market identity — brand naming, website, label compliance, social media presence — so Chinese buyers find you immediately credible.
03
Market Access
Members gain access to Norenfoods' existing premium retail network, joint exhibition opportunities at SIAL China and FHC Shanghai, and a dedicated channel via our curated shelf program.
Selection Criteria

What it takes
to join the alliance

We set the bar high — because the Noren Selected mark must mean something to every Chinese buyer who sees it.

I
International Award Recognition
Verified recognition in Flos Olei, NYIOOC World Olive Oil Competition, or Gambero Rosso. We weight consistency across multiple years and categories. One-time wins count; multi-year excellence counts more.
II
GACC Registration
Valid registration with China's General Administration of Customs (GACC) as an overseas food manufacturer — or actively in the registration process. Not registered yet? We can guide you through the process.
III
China Market Readiness
Completion of BrandBank localization — Chinese brand identity, compliant labeling, and a credible digital presence for Chinese buyers. This is what transforms great oil into a great Chinese brand.
IV
Partnership Alignment
Willingness to work with Norenfoods as your dedicated China market partner. We provide end-to-end services — from import compliance and localization to channel placement and brand building — so you can focus on making great oil.
